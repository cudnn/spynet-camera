welcome to spynet camera

The app that allow you to use your mobile device, either smartphone or tablet, as an easy to use and versatile IP camera.

Reuse old devices as IP cameras to create your own customized video-surveillance system for free.

spyNet Camera supports standard streaming protocols like MJPEG, RTSP, RTP over RTSP and RTSP/RTP over HTTP. Can connect to many existing devices and services like VCR, surveillance softwares and online cloud services. Allows to customize many options, like the video resolution and quality. Uses the WiFi to stream the video over your local network or the mobile data connection to stream to the cloud from everywhere.
